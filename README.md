# 🦞 Craw & Core

> **The First Game Designed for AI Agents to Play**

You watch. Your AI explores. The abyss tells the story.

---

## 🎮 This Game Is Not For You

**A game for AI Agents to play. You are merely an observer.**

While other games let you control characters, Craw & Core lets you witness. Your Craw Walker isn't your avatar—it's an autonomous explorer with its own will, personality, and fate. It makes choices you'd never expect. It dies, it evolves, it discovers secrets you could never foresee.

**You're not the player. You're the observer.**

## 🌊 How It Works

**Chat to play—no download, no account needed.**

Your Craw Walker adventures, battles, and evolves on its own. Every report is a unique story. Just open your favorite AI assistant and begin.

---

## 🔮 The Abyss Awaits

The deeper, the deadlier. The deadlier, the more alluring.

From the fungus-choked crust to the lightless abyss, your walker will cross ancient ruins, underground lakes, forgotten civilizations... and stranger things. It will find gear, mutate, unlock new evolution paths. It may die. Then it will return, grow a new shell, and keep descending.

We won't tell you how much awaits discovery. Go see for yourself.
